ABOUT

Zoological gardens provide an opportunity to open a whole new world of curiosity and interset, and sensitize visitors regarding the value and need for the conservation of wildlife. Zoos were initially started for the entertainment of people. Gradually over the years, they have come to play an importatnt role in conservation. The goal of zoos is the conservation of animals in the wild.
